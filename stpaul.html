Experience Saint Paul:

Below we have compiled a list of some of our favorite attractions in the city of Saint Paul.

  1. Minnesota State Capitol: Take a tour of the Minnesota State Capitol, an iconic building with a dome that stands out on the city skyline. Learn about the state's history and government while appreciating the architecture.

  2. Cathedral of Saint Paul:Visit the Cathedral of Saint Paul, a stunning architectural masterpiece. This Roman Catholic cathedral sits on Summit Hill and offers guided tours showcasing its history and art.

  3. Como Park Zoo and Conservatory: Explore Como Park, which houses a zoo with a variety of animals and a beautiful conservatory featuring an array of plants. Admission to the park is free, making it a great family-friendly destination.

  4. Science Museum of Minnesota: Visit the Science Museum of Minnesota, located on the banks of the Mississippi River. The museum features interactive exhibits on science, technology, and natural history, making it an engaging experience for visitors of all ages.

  5. Mississippi Riverwalk: Enjoy a stroll along the Mississippi Riverwalk, which provides picturesque views of the river and the downtown skyline. The area is often bustling with activities, and you can find parks, restaurants, and scenic spots along the way.

Saint Paul, Minnesota, on the banks of the Mississippi River, harmonizes historic elegance with modern vibrancy. Home to the state capitol and cultural landmarks, the city offers a rich tapestry of theaters, museums, and outdoor escapes. Embrace the charm, community warmth, and diverse experiences that define this dynamic Midwestern gem.
